# BANDHA Boutique Curator Design System

## Core Identity: "The Editorial Bazaar"
An artisanal, exclusive, and warm aesthetic that feels like a curated community magazine or a high-end designer boutique. This design departs from generic marketplace patterns to establish BANDHA as a unique "curated community."

### 1. Vision & Tone
- **Atmosphere**: Warm, artisanal, high-end editorial, human-centric.
- **Goal**: Create a unique, trustworthy space that feels "curated" rather than "industrial."
- **Key Concepts**: Serif typography, generous whitespace, thin elegant borders, and earthy foundations.

### 2. Color Palette
- **Charcoal (Primary)**: `#1A1A1A` (Text, Logo, Strong contrast).
- **Warm Off-White (Foundation)**: `#FCFBFA` (Canvas background, Airy feel).
- **Muted Camel (Accent)**: `#D4A373` (Interaction cues, Hover states).
- **Soft Stone (Secondary)**: `#F1F0EE` (Surface cards, Subheads).
- **Sage (Success)**: `#4A5D4E` (Savings, Positive status).

### 3. Typography
- **Headings**: **Playfair Display** (Serif) - Elegant, establishing authority and curation.
- **Body**: **Inter** or **Plus Jakarta Sans** (Sans) - Clean, high utility.
- **Weights**: Playfair Bold (700) for headers, Inter Medium (500) for UI.

### 4. Surfaces & Geometry
- **Gallery Cards**: Pure white surfaces on warm-white backgrounds. Minimalist 16px corners.
- **Borders**: Hairline borders (`0.5px`) in Stone (#E5E5E5).
- **Shadows**: Barely perceptible layered shadows.
  - `shadow-boutique`: `0 10px 15px -3px rgb(0 0 0 / 0.02), 0 4px 6px -4px rgb(0 0 0 / 0.02)`

### 5. UI Components
- **Header**: Slim, 64px height. Pure white. Serif logo centered or left-aligned.
- **Buttons**: Outlined or solid Charcoal. Subtle rounding (8px) for an artisanal feel.
- **Inputs**: Minimalist borders. Focused, clean.
- **Product Cards**: Emphasis on high-quality photography. Serif titles. Thin, elegant "Savings" badges.

---

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 15.1
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS v4
- **Fonts**: Playfair Display (via Google Fonts), Inter.

> [!TIP]
> Always prioritize whitespace. The "Boutique" look relies on things feeling uncrowded and intentional.
